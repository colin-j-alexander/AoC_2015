A quick and dirty solution would be to count the nos of ‘(’ and ‘)’ and
then take the difference to determine the floor.

``` r
up   <- str_count(input, "\\(")
down <- str_count(input, "\\)")
floor <- up - down 
```

Santa goes `up =` 3569 floors and `down =` 3431 floors taking him to
`floor =` 138.
